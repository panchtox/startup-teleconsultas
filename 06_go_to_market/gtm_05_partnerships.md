### Deal Registration Process

**Why Deal Registration?**
- Protects partner's investment in pursuing deal
- Avoids channel conflict (multiple partners competing for same customer)
- Incentivizes partners to bring new opportunities

**Process:**
1. Partner identifies opportunity
2. Submits deal registration form:
   - Customer name + contact
   - Estimated deal size
   - Expected close date
   - Partner's relationship with customer
3. ConectaSalud reviews within 48 hours
4. If approved:
   - Deal "locked" to that partner for 90 days
   - Other partners blocked from pursuing
   - Partner gets full commission if closes
5. If rejected:
   - Reason provided (e.g., already in pipeline, outside territory)
   - Partner can appeal or pursue non-exclusively
